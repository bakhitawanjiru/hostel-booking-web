Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Royal Crest Hostel, our rooms, facilities, and booking process.

Booking is easy! Simply visit our Rooms page, select your preferred room type, click "View Details", and follow the booking instructions. You'll need to pay a deposit to secure your reservation, with the balance due on arrival or via bank transfer.

We accept bank transfers, M-Pesa, cash payments on arrival, and online transfers. For booking deposits, you can use any of these methods. Contact our team for specific payment details and available options.

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 7 days before your arrival. A cancellation fee may apply depending on how close it is to your check-in date. Contact our support team for assistance.

All room rentals include access to high-speed WiFi, furnished bedding, 24/7 security, utilities (water and electricity), and access to all hostel facilities including the gym, study areas, shared kitchen, and lounge.

Yes! High-speed WiFi is included in all room packages. Our network is optimized for streaming, online classes, and gaming. Most students get speeds of 50+ Mbps on average.

Our house rules include quiet hours from 10 PM to 8 AM, no smoking inside rooms, no loud parties, respect for shared facilities, and basic cleanliness standards. Guests are expected to follow these rules to ensure everyone's comfort and safety.

Yes! We have a dedicated laundry room with washing machines available daily from 6 AM to 8 PM. There's a small fee per load. We also offer laundry services if you prefer professional cleaning.

Yes! Royal Crest Hostel is strategically located in Nairobi with easy access to major universities and colleges. We're within walking distance of several campuses and close to public transport routes for quick commutes.

We prioritize guest safety with 24/7 security staff, CCTV cameras in common areas, secure room locks, gated compound, and controlled access. Each room has individual lockers for valuables. Guests are provided with security tips upon arrival.

Yes, guests are welcome during daytime visiting hours (8 AM to 8 PM). Overnight guests require advance notification and management approval. Please respect quiet hours and other residents' privacy.

Our Studio Room is perfect for solo students who want privacy. The Double Room is great for two people sharing. The 4-Person Room is budget-friendly for groups. Our Premium Deluxe Suite offers luxury with double space, Smart TV, and en-suite bathroom. Choose based on your needs and budget!

You can reach us via email, phone, or visit our Contact page to submit an inquiry. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours. For urgent matters, call our reception desk available 24/7.
